Why We Built Solance

Solana users deserve a simple way to visualize their assets without needing technical skills.

We're a team of student developers who believe in the Solana ecosystem but found existing portfolio trackers too complex. Our goal is to create the simplest possible experience - like "Google Analytics for your Solana wallet."

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Solance free to use?

Yes, the basic features will always be free. We plan to introduce premium features in the future for advanced users.

Do I need to connect my wallet?

No. Simply enter your wallet address and we'll fetch the public data from the blockchain. We never ask for or store your private keys.

Which Solana assets can I track?

All SPL tokens, NFTs from Metaplex, and staking rewards from protocols like Marinade and Socean.

Will you support other blockchains?

We're starting with Solana, but plan to expand to other chains like BSC, Base, and Ethereum in the future.
